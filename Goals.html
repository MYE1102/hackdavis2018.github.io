Our Goals

"In November 2013, UC President Napolitano announced the UC Carbon Neutrality Initiative to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions from all campus activities, buildings and vehicles by 2025." (UC Davis Campus Tomorrow)

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to minimize the energy usage by comparing LEED certified buildings to non-LEED-certified buildings.

And give posssible recommendation to improve on saving energy of the non-LEED certified buildings.
